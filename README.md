# Global War Impact Analysis 2026 – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Power BI dashboard and relational dataset** designed to analyze the potential global economic, geopolitical, and humanitarian impacts of a hypothetical **2026 Iran–United States–Israel conflict scenario**. The objective is to demonstrate advanced data modeling, business intelligence, and visualization capabilities by examining how large-scale geopolitical events can influence multiple sectors across the world.

The dashboard consolidates data from various interconnected domains and provides an interactive environment for exploring the ripple effects of global conflict on energy markets, trade, inflation, defense spending, economic growth, refugee movements, and other critical indicators.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze the global impact of a major geopolitical conflict.
* Build a scalable relational data model in Power BI.
* Create interactive visualizations for cross-sector analysis.
* Demonstrate data storytelling using business intelligence techniques.
* Provide insights into economic, social, and strategic consequences of war.

---

## Dataset Architecture

The project uses a **star-schema relational model** consisting of:

### Dimension Tables

* **Dim_Countries** – Country-level information, regions, population, and GDP.
* **Dim_Dates** – Date hierarchy for time-series analysis.

### Fact Tables

* Oil Prices
* LPG Prices
* LNG Trade
* Shipping & Logistics
* Inflation
* Defense Spending
* Stock Market Performance
* Currency Exchange Impact
* Commodities
* Refugee Movement
* Cyber Attacks
* International Sanctions
* Aviation Industry Impact
* Global Trade
* Energy Sector Impact
* Food Supply Chain
* Employment Trends
* GDP Growth Forecast

The dataset contains multiple fact tables connected through **Country_ID** and **Date_ID**, enabling multidimensional analysis across sectors and time periods.
